Hi Kiran,

The problem is that org.apache.jmeter:ApacheJMeter:2.11 declares a
dependency (in its parent POM: org.apache.jmeter:ApacheJMeter_parent:2.11)
on rsyntaxtextarea version 2.5.1, which has not available on Central yet:

http://search.maven.org/#search%7Cgav%7C1%7Cg%3A%22com.fifesoft%22%20AND%20a%3A%22rsyntaxtextarea%22

It declares the version in a property, so I was thinking you could fix it
in your POM by adding:

<properties>
<rsyntaxtextarea.version>2.5.0</rsyntaxtextarea.version>
</properties>

But when I tested it, it didn't work for some reason. (Which is odd: I use
the same trick for many of my projects, and it works just fine...)

But anyway, it is a problem with JMeter: they shouldn't use an unavailable
version of a dependency.

Regards,
Curtis
