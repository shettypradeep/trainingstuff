Hi Kiran,

I was able to make your POM work by also explicitly adding the
rsyntaxtextarea dependency, which overrides the version:

    <dependency>
      <groupId>com.fifesoft</groupId>
      <artifactId>rsyntaxtextarea</artifactId>
      <version>${rsyntaxtextarea.version}</version>
      <scope>runtime</scope>
    </dependency>

HTH,
Curtis
